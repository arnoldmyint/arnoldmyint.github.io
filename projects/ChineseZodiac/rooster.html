The Year of the Rooster

The Rooster is tenth in the Chinese zodiac. Each year is related to an animal sign according to a 12-year cycle. The 12 zodiac animals are: Rat, Ox, Tiger, Rabbit, Dragon, Snake, Horse, Goat, Monkey, Rooster, Dog, and Pig

  • Rooster Years: 1921, 1933, 1945, 1957, 1969, 1981, 1993, 2005, 2017, and 2029
  • Lucky Colors: Gold, Brown, Yellow
  • Lucky Numbers: 5, 7, and 8
  • Lucky Flowers: Gladiola, Cockscomb
