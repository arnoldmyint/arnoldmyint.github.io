The Year of the Monkey

The monkey ranks ninth of the 12 animals in the Chinese zodiac. Each year is related to an animal sign according to a 12-year-cycle. The 12 zodiac animals are: Rat, Ox, Tiger, Rabbit, Dragon, Snake, Horse, Goat, Monkey, Rooster, Dog, and Pig

  • Monkey Years: 1920, 1932, 1944, 1956, 1968, 1980, 1992, 2004, 2016, 2028
  • Lucky Colors: White, Blue, Gold
  • Lucky Numbers: 4, 9
  • Lucky Flowers: chrysanthemum, crape-myrtle
